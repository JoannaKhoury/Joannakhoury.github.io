Digital Transformation – Real Estate Team

 

The Challenge

Acquiring high-quality leads is costly, and despite all efforts many slipped through the cracks due to limited follow-up capacity.

Brokers, while dedicated, lacked the time and tools to maximize every opportunity.

 

The Approach

The focus was to help the brokers and the team, follow up with the leads on time, to avoid losing leads, and increase sales.

The team implemented Follow Up Boss, the specialized CRM for real estate companies in US and Canada and my role was to make this implementation smooth and beneficial.

 

 

The Solution

·       Deployed and tailored a new CRM system to fit in-house needs.

·       Optimized the process with clear phases, rules and smart lists.

·       Introduced Inside Sales Agents (ISAs) to improve lead handling.

·       Build a structured onboarding process that helps scaling.

·       Provide training and hands-on support to ensure smooth adoption.

 

 

Useful Features

 

Comprehensive leads profile that contain all the communication history.

 

Smart lists to follow up with clients on time

 

Calendar to allow for efficient collaboration

 

Deals funnel to allow forecasting

 

More Insights

CRM customization proved essential to success. Each team works differently and this needs to be taken into account and respected when implementing new tools or optimizing a process.

Change needs to be done incrementally and have all team members on boarded. brokers and all team members where cooperative and open to change.

Clarifying the benefits of the new system, how it works and how it can help the team is crucial for a smooth adoption.

 

 

 

 

The Impact

 

Agents can easily follow up with all leads

Leads follow up increased by 50%

Management was able to scale from 12 to 30 brokers smoothly

Better visibility, service and follow up with the Buyers and Sellers

Closed deals increase by 25%

National rank of the team goes from 34 to Top 19 over 300 teams

The team becomes among the Top 50 worldwide list
